Prompting Guide: Style Transform

This tool uses your uploaded image as a style reference to create a brand new image based on your prompt.

  1. Upload an image: This image defines the artistic style (e.g., watercolor, 3D render, cartoon).
  2. Write a prompt: Describe the new content you want to create (e.g., "a futuristic car," "a queen on a throne").

The AI will combine the style from your image with the content from your prompt.

Example:

Using this art style, create an image of a majestic lion wearing a crown.

Pro Tips:

  • A clear, well-defined style in your uploaded image will produce better results.
  • Be descriptive about the new content.
  • Adjust the Width and Height sliders to match the desired shape of your *new* image.
