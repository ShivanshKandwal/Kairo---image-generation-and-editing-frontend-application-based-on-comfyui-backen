Prompting Guide: Image Transform

This tool transforms the style of an image based on your prompt. The key is to describe the target style and what to preserve.

Line Art to Full Color

Describe the final, colored scene in detail. Mention colors, lighting, textures, and the overall mood.

Transform this line art into a detailed digital painting. Give the character a red hat and blue shirt. The background should be a sunny forest with soft lighting.


Full Color to Line Art

Specify the desired line style. "Clean vector" is different from "sketchy charcoal".

Convert this photo to a clean black and white coloring page. Use consistent, bold outlines and remove all shading.


Photo to Watercolor

Describe the artistic characteristics of a watercolor painting.

Change this photo into a watercolor painting. Use soft, blended colors with visible paper texture and wet-on-wet brush strokes.
