Prompting Guide - Image-to-Image

Specify what you want to change and the AI will follow. It understands the context of the image, making edits easy without overly detailed descriptions.

Basic Object Modifications

The AI is great at straightforward changes. To change the color of an object, simply ask for it.

Change the yellow car to a red car.


Prompt Precision

For more complex edits, be more explicit to ensure you get the desired result while preserving the original style.

  • Simple Edit: Change to daytime (May alter the style).
  • Controlled Edit: Change to daytime while maintaining the same style of the painting. (Preserves the original look).

Character & Subject Consistency

To keep a character looking the same across edits, be very specific about what should not change.

  1. Establish the reference: "The woman with short black hair..."
  2. State the change: "...is now on a beach."
  3. Preserve identity: "...while maintaining her exact facial features and expression."
